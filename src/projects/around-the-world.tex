\headedsubsection % Job title entry for the current employer
{\myhref{https://github.com/cderici/around-the-world-of-compilers}{Around the World of Compilers}}
{}
{\small\bodytext{
Collection of small experimental compilers, one for each letter of the alphabet, named after a world city. Some work on the frontend (e.g. \acr{MLIR.smt} + \acr{Z3} based verification), and some focus on the backend (e.g. \acr{LLVM} instruction selection and later passes). Each explores different ideas in language design, \acr{IR} construction, optimizations, and run-time. Targets include \acr{LLVM IR}, \acr{MLIR}, \acr{NVPTX}. All in C++.
}}
